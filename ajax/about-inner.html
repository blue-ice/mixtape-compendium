Have you ever listened to an audiocassette mixtape?

Yes? Then you will understand the warmth, the humanity, and the vulnerability found in a small plastic casing that you can fit in the palm of your hand.

No? Then you have so much to look forward to: mixtapes are music playlists stolen from an era, but they are more than just relics- they are views into the recorder's mind. They provide a perfectly imperfect music listening experience, with a human touch and all of its associated human flaws.

This is what makes audiocassette mixtapes so interesting, and also so rare- the music that we find currently comes in playlists cooked up by computer algorithms. Why not listen to a playlist carefully composed by a human being?

What are you waiting for? pick a mixtape and put on those 90's headphones!
